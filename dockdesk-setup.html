How to set up call forwarding

1

iPhone

Go to Settings → Phone → Call Forwarding. Turn it on and enter your Dock Desk number. Or dial *21*[number]# to forward all calls.

2

Android

Go to Phone app → Settings → Call Forwarding. Set "Forward when unanswered" to your Dock Desk number.

3

Quick toggle for on the water

Before you leave the dock, just dial *72[DockDeskNumber] to enable forwarding. When you're back, dial *73 to disable it. Takes 5 seconds.
